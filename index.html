About DR Ceylon Tours

Your trusted travel partner in Sri Lanka since 2016

Our Story

Founded in 2016, DR Ceylon Tours began with a simple mission: to provide reliable, affordable, and professional transportation services to travelers exploring the beautiful island of Sri Lanka.

What started as a small family-run taxi service with just one vehicle has grown into one of Sri Lanka's most trusted tour and taxi companies, serving thousands of satisfied customers from around the world.

Today, we operate a modern fleet of vehicles and employ over 50 professional drivers, all dedicated to making your Sri Lankan experience unforgettable. Our drivers are not just chauffeurs – they're knowledgeable guides who share their love for Sri Lanka with every passenger.

Our Mission

To provide safe, reliable, and affordable transportation and tour services that exceed our customers' expectations. We strive to showcase the beauty and culture of Sri Lanka while ensuring every journey is comfortable, enjoyable, and memorable.

Our Vision

To become Sri Lanka's leading tour and taxi service provider, recognized for excellence in customer service, safety standards, and contribution to sustainable tourism. We envision a future where every visitor's first choice for Sri Lankan travel is DR Ceylon Tours.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a taxi or tour?

Booking is easy! You can book via WhatsApp (+94 70 191 4681), phone call, or by filling out the contact form above. We'll respond immediately with availability and pricing.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash (LKR, USD, EUR), bank transfers, and credit/debit cards. Payment can be made before or after the service, as per mutual agreement.

Are your prices fixed or negotiable?

We offer competitive fixed pricing with no hidden charges. For long-term bookings or group tours, special discounts may be available. Contact us for a custom quote.

Do you provide airport pickup at night?

Yes! We operate 24/7, including late night and early morning airport pickups. Just share your flight details and we'll be there on time.

Can I customize my tour itinerary?

Absolutely! All our tour packages are flexible and can be customized according to your interests, time, and budget. Just let us know your preferences.

Are your drivers English-speaking?

Yes, all our drivers speak English fluently. Many also speak Tamil, Sinhala, and have basic knowledge of other languages.
