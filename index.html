Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

How do I book a taxi or tour?

You can book easily through WhatsApp by clicking the "Book Now" button, calling us directly at +94 70 191 4681, or filling out the contact form on our website. We respond instantly on WhatsApp!

Are your vehicles and drivers licensed?

Yes! All our vehicles are registered and insured. Our drivers hold valid licenses and are approved by the Sri Lanka Tourism Development Authority. We maintain the highest safety standards.

Do you provide airport pickup services?

Yes! We offer 24/7 airport transfer services from Bandaranaike International Airport. Our driver will meet you at the arrival gate with a name board and assist with your luggage.

Are your vehicles air-conditioned?

Yes, all our vehicles are fully air-conditioned, well-maintained, and regularly serviced for your comfort and safety.

Can I customize my tour package?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating personalized itineraries based on your interests, budget, and travel dates. Contact us to discuss your custom tour.

What is your cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 24 hours before your scheduled service. For cancellations within 24 hours, a 50% charge applies. Emergency cancellations are handled case-by-case.
